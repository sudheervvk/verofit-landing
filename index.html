About Us

Built by lifelong athletes turned engineers, we know what it feels like to train, compete, and strive for more. After years in software jobs, we returned to our passion—sports—to create a tool we wish we had: an adaptive AI coach that understands athletes.

This is not just an app. It’s a movement to help everyday athletes unlock professional-level training with the power of AI.

FAQs

1. What makes this different from other fitness apps?

+
Most fitness apps give you static plans or generic workouts. Our platform uses adaptive AI that learns from your performance, fatigue, and goals—so your plan evolves as you do. It’s like having a personal coach in your pocket, but smarter and available 24/7.

2. Do I need to be a professional athlete to use this?

+
Not at all! Whether you’re a weekend runner, a competitive amateur, or aiming for professional-level performance, the system adjusts to your level. Beginners get structured guidance, while advanced athletes benefit from progressive periodization and smarter load management.

3. How does the AI actually work?

+
Our AI analyzes your training logs, performance data, and goals to create tailored drills and progression. Over time, it detects patterns in how you recover, adapt, and perform—so your training becomes increasingly personal and effective. Think of it as a coach that never stops learning about you.

4. Is this just about fitness, or does it cover recovery and nutrition too?

+
Our first launch focuses on sport-specific training plans and adaptive fitness. Recovery and nutrition insights are on our roadmap, but we believe in doing one thing exceptionally well first: helping athletes train smarter for lasting results.

5. Will my data be safe?

+
Absolutely. Your performance data is yours. We use industry-standard encryption and never sell your personal information. Our mission is to empower athletes, not exploit their data.
